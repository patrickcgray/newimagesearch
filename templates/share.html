Are you ready for Atlasaur?


We are finding and sharing the world's adventures. We want it to be really easy for anyone to find the best outdoor activities and to make it convenient for them to keep track of their adventures and share with friends.


Site Layout:

  • Search for Adventure - your go to place for finding adventure anytime anywhere
  • The Community - check out fellow adventurers pictures, groups, and discover some recent adventures
  • After logging in you will gain access to:
    • Profile - the adventures you have gone on, the ones you want to go on, and all your activity
    • Groups - you can create and join groups to share your adventures with others
    • Forum - read and contribute to the forum which has exciting and up to date stories, photos, and videos

Other tips:

  • After registering you will gain the ability to share your own adventures, rate other reviews, and join the discussion
  • Get excited! and find some awesome things to do.
  • Let us know if you think of anything we should add at mgmt@atlasaur.com
