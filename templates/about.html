F.A.Q.


What form is the OBX Vacation Hero Guide delivered in?

-Upon successful purchase you will be provided a link to download a PDF of the Guide. From this PDF you are able to print any of the specific deals you would like to use or just print the whole Guide.


Why pay for these deals when many companies offer coupons and discounts for free?

Our purpose is to make your vacation more enjoyable and less expensive, so we have a collection of deals that save you more than $30 each and are organized in a such a way to make it easy to find just what you want and provide ideas for more exciting activities to do on the OBX.
